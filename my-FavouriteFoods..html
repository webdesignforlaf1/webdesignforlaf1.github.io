My Favorite Foods

Growing up in Guyana, my favorite restaurant was Youngs

My favourite dishes were

  1. Fried Rice
  2. Chow Mein
  3. Stir Fry Okra

Ingredients of my Favorite Foods

The Ingredients for each of these meals were

Spicy Stir Fried Okra

  1. 1 tbsp sesame oil
  2. 1 - 5 small chilli peppers (according to taste)
  3. 8 oz fresh Okra
  4. 1 tbsp Chinese Five Spices
  5. 5 choppped spring onions
  6. 5 cloves garlic

Caution: Be cautious with the chillies - use the apprtopriate number acccording to your tolerance of spicy food.

Otherwise your mouth will catch fire and you will jump in the river to cool down
