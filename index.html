My Favorite Foods in Guyana

Growing up in Guyana, my favourite dishes were

  1. Fried Rice
  2. Chow Mein
  3. Spicy Stir Fry Cabbage

Ingredients of my Favorite Foods

The Ingredients for each of these meals were

Spicy Stir Fried Cabbage

  1. 1 tbsp sesame oil
  2. 1 - 5 small chilli peppers (according to taste)
  3. 8 oz fresh Cabbage
  4. 1 tbsp Chinese Five Spices
  5. 5 choppped onions
  6. 5 cloves garlic

Caution: Be cautious with the chillies - use the appropriate number acccording to your tolerance of spicy food.

Otherwise your mouth will catch fire and you will jump in the river to cool down
